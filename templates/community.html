Get Involved



7 things you can do today to start learning or volunteer in a diverse community of coders


1. Attend an event with us
Intimidated by the hackathon and meet-up scene? Rather go with a friend than show up alone? Get in touch with us to find out which tech events we're attending and make plans to attend with one of our staff or fellows.


2. Mentor a fellow
Invest in the success of a Code for Progress Fellow by volunteering as a one-on-one mentor.


3. Teach or coach on a skill
Spend some time in the classroom running a workshop in your area of expertise - like resume building, networking, project management, or a coding skill.


4. Make an introduction
Know of the perfect future fellow, partner organization, or guest instructor? Let us know!


5. Get updates
Stay up to date on the latest Code for Progress news.


6. Donate
Make a one-time donation or sign up to give every month.


7. Volunteer another way
Have a passion for tech and social change but need a different way to channel it? Send us a note and we'll help you find a way that works.
