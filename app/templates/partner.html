Our community has grown because of the work we've done alongside organizations, agencies, foundations, and companies like yours. We look for partners to help us build and diversify the tech workforce pipeline in three ways.



Hire a Graduate

Code for Progress fellows move into full time employment after the residency period. Find out which fellows are looking for employers like you and help us build the pipeline into coding work for women and people of color.


Sponsor a Fellow

Code for Progress supports its fellows through holistic training and support, and through a stipend that covers all housing, living, and travel expenses during the five-month residency. Contact our senior staff to discuss how you can support a fellow from recruitment to graduation.


Build a Project

Code for Progress fellows and staff have built sites and apps for organizations with social justice or civic engagement missions. Get in touch with us to find out how your staff or volunteers can learn about social justice tech while building tools alongside people like them.
