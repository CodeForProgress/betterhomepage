Executive Director

Michelle Fox



Michelle Fox is the first generation child of a Haitian immigrant turned proud SEIU 1199 member, who taught lessons of feminism and social and economic justice at the dinner table.

Michelle began her career as an unofficial campus organizer and jumped on her first campaign shortly after graduation. She wandered these United States for a few years with a band of merry field organizers, and settled in DC to work in the administration of Mayor Adrian Fenty. Most recently, she was the VP of Operations for a non-political tech-start up based in DC.

Michelle comes to Code for Progress with a passion for social justice and fostering economic mobility, and a wealth of organizing and operations experience. She lives in Washington DC with her partner, Maddy, and their dog, Zoey Fox Melton.
