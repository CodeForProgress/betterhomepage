The application process

In 2014, 424 people applied to the Code for Progress fellowship. We expect even more interest in 2015, and our selection committee will get to know candidates through a two-round process.

Round 1 includes an online application where candidates answer short questions and ask three recommenders to do the same. Candidates who move on to Round 2 will join our staff for two video interviews.

We'll hold informational calls to answer your questions every Thursday night From February 19 to March 26, from 8-8:30pm Eastern / 5-5:30pm Pacific. To request the dial-in number, please send us a note here.
