Our Mission

Code For Progress brings women and people of color into the coding workforce through holistic investment in their technical training while providing communities of support.

What is Code for Progress?

Code For Progress is a nonprofit organization that offers formalized coding training programs to women and people of color. We are based in Washington, DC and work with students, professionals, and organizations nationwide.

What do we do?

Code For Progress offers a yearlong web development fellowship that includes an intensive 5-month instructor-led classroom training followed by a 6-month internship with one of our internship host partner organizations. Through the internship program participants get the opportunity refine their tech skills in a real-world work environment.

Code For Progress also offers the Community Learning Series (CLS), an online training program that was developed to bring programming fundamentals to a broader audience. The CLS includes a list of Front-End and Back-End web development courses that will be offered through our online-training platform throughout the year.
