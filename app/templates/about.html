Our Mission

Code For Progress brings women and people of color into the coding workforce through holistic investment in their technical training while providing communities of support.





| What is Code for Progress?

Code For Progress is a nonprofit organization that offers formalized coding training programs to women and people of color. We are based in Washington, DC and work with students, professionals, and organizations nationwide.

| What do we do?

Code For Progress offers a yearlong web development fellowship that includes an intensive 5-month instructor-led classroom training followed by a 6-month internship with one of our internship host partner organizations.
